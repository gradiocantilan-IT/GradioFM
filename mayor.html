Why We Support Digoy Eleazar

Vice Mayor Rodrigo "Digoy" Eleazar of Cantilan, Surigao del Sur, is a dedicated public servant committed to the progress and welfare of Cantilangnons. Elected as Vice Mayor in the 2025 elections with 9,685 votes, Digoy has been a steadfast leader, supporting initiatives for community development, education, and infrastructure in Cantilan.[](https://peoplaid.com/2025/06/04/cantilan-2025-election-results-winners/)

His leadership, alongside Mayor Philip Arreza Pichay, has driven projects like the Lobo to Santiago Road Project and strengthened disaster preparedness through the Incident Command System (ICS) Executive Course. Join us in supporting Vice Mayor Digoy as he continues to serve Cantilan with integrity and vision![](https://cantilan.gov.ph/)
