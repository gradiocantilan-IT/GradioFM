Why We Support Gov. Johnny

Governor Johnny T. Pimentel, a seasoned leader and public servant, has dedicated his career to uplifting Surigao del Sur. Elected as governor in 2025, he previously served as the province’s governor from 2010 to 2016 and as a representative of Surigao del Sur’s 2nd District from 2016 to 2025. His leadership has brought significant progress, including infrastructure development, peace initiatives, and improved public services.

Known for his role in facilitating the release of captives during his tenure and his legislative contributions, including authoring 72 enacted laws, Governor Johnny continues to champion the welfare of Surigaonons. Join us in supporting his vision for a prosperous and peaceful Surigao del Sur!
